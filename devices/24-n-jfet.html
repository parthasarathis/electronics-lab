About N-Channel JFET
• Voltage-controlled device (very high input impedance)
• Pinch-off constricts channel at gate-source junction
• Shichman-Hodges: Id = Idss(1−Vgs/Vp)² for |Vgs| < |Vp|
• Common examples: 2N4124, 2N5457, J176
• Transconductance: gm = −2Idss(1−Vgs/Vp)/Vp
• Output resistance: ro ≈ VA/Idss (Early voltage ≈ 100 V)
