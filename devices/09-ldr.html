Teaching Points
· LDR uses CdS (Cadmium Sulfide) as photosensitive material
· Resistance drops from MΩ (dark) to kΩ (bright)
· γ determines sensitivity slope on log-log graph
· Response time: ~10ms (slower than photodiodes)
· Used in light-activated switches and cameras
· Voltage divider: increase R₂ for higher Vout at low light
