Teaching Points
· Rectification: converts AC to pulsating DC via one-way conduction
· Forward voltage Vf ≈ 0.6–0.7V (Si), nearly constant (ideal diode ≈ 0V)
· Shockley equation: I = Is(e^(V/Vt) - 1) governs I-V curve
· Peak Inverse Voltage (PIV) = max reverse bias; must exceed supply peak
· Ripple voltage inversely proportional to capacitor & resistance
· Recovery time trr ≈ 100–1000 ns limits switching frequency

Rectifier Types
· 1N4007: 1A, 1000V (general-purpose)
· 1N4148: 200mA, 75V (fast switching)
· UF4007: ultra-fast recovery
· Schottky: lower Vf, faster (0.3V)
