About Varistors (MOVs)
• Metal Oxide: ZnO + Bi₂O₃, very nonlinear
• Voltage range: 14V–680V nominal
• Nonlinearity: α ≈ 0.25 (1/4 power law)
• Energy rating: 10–10k J (surge pulse)
• Degradation: Each surge reduces lifespan
• Clamping ratio: 1.5–3× rated voltage
