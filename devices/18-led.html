Teaching Points
· Forward-biased P-N junction: electron-hole recombination emits photons
· Bandgap energy Eg determines color: hν ≈ Eg (red ~1.9V, blue ~3.4V)
· Forward voltage Vf ≈ Eg/e, nearly independent of If (ideal diode)
· Luminous efficiency η = Popt/(Vf·If), typically 5-40% depending on color
· Temperature increases Vf slightly (≈ -2mV/°C), blue LEDs more temperature-sensitive
· Series resistor needed: Rs ≈ (Vsupply - Vf) / If (e.g., 100Ω for 5V, 20mA)
