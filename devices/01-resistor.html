Key Teaching Points
① V = I·R — Ohm's Law: current and voltage are linearly proportional.
② Power heats the resistor: P = I²R = V²/R. Must stay below the power rating.
③ Temperature coefficient: metal film ~100ppm/°C; carbon ~3000ppm/°C.
④ Color code: 4 bands — digit, digit, multiplier, tolerance (gold=±5%).
⑤ Series: RT = R₁+R₂+…  |  Parallel: 1/RT = 1/R₁+1/R₂+…
⑥ Conductance G = 1/R measured in Siemens (S) — ease of current flow.
⑦ Standard E12/E24/E96 series: 12, 24, or 96 preferred values per decade.
