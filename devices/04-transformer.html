About Transformers
• Ideal transformer: Lossless, perfect coupling
• Core loss: Hysteresis + eddy current losses

• Isolation voltage: Primary-secondary galvanic isolation
• EI core: Cheap, common in power supply
• Toroidal: Low EMI, high efficiency, quiet
