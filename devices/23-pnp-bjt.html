About PNP BJT
• Complementary pair with NPN BJT
• Used in push-pull amplifiers & power stages
• α = β/(β+1) ≈ 0.99 for high-β devices
• High-β examples: BC559, 2N3906
• Current flows: Emitter → Collector (conventional)
• Temperature: β increases ~0.5%/°C
