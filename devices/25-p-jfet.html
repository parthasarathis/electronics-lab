About P-Channel JFET
• Complementary to N-channel JFET
• Hole-conducting channel, positive Vp and Vgs
• Used in complementary output stages & push-pull
• Shichman-Hodges: Id = Idss(1−Vgs/Vp)² for Vgs < Vp
• Common examples: 2N3688, 2N5460
• Output resistance: ro ≈ VA/Idss (Early voltage ~100 V)
